Frequently Asked Questions
about Herbal Skincare

Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes, our herbal skincare products are formulated using natural ingredients, making them safe and effective for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Do your products contain any harmful chemicals?

No, all our products are free from parabens, sulfates, and other harmful chemicals. We believe in clean beauty powered by nature.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! We never test on animals and are proud to be a cruelty-free brand.

Can I use these products daily?

Yes, our products are gentle enough for daily use and are designed to provide consistent, long-term benefits for your skin.

How long does it take to see results?

While results may vary by individual, most customers notice visible improvements within 2–3 weeks of regular use.

What if I have an allergic reaction?

We recommend doing a patch test before using any new skincare product. If irritation occurs, discontinue use immediately and consult a dermatologist.
